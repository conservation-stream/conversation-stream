How Nested Matrices Work

  • Outer Matrix (App-level): The determine-changes job produces a matrix with 2 apps: kb and site. Each app becomes a separate workflow run.
  • Inner Matrix (Build-level): The kb app has a build_matrix with architectures amd64 and arm64. This creates 2 parallel build jobs within that app's workflow.
  • Fan-out ‚Üí Fan-in: Build jobs fan out (parallel execution), then deploy waits for ALL matrix builds to complete before running (fan-in).
  • Total Jobs: 1 determine + 3 builds (2 for kb, 1 for site) + 2 deploys = 6 jobs
